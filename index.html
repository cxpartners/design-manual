The cxpartners logo

This is the latest version of the cxpartners logo.

Minimum size

The smallest the cx logo should go is 100px, or equivalent. Below that legibility issues may occur.

Spacing

Give the logo space to breathe, and make sure nothing encroaches within the area defined above.

Colours and overlays

The logo can be used in a variety of ways:

On a white background

When on white or cx grey, please use the full colour version of the logo.

On a dark background

When using the cx logo on a dark background colour (preferably cx grey), please use the white version of the logo.

On a photo background

When on a photo background, please use the white version of the logo and ensure legibility by using a 40% opacity overlay.

What not to do

Don’t distort / squish the logo, at all ever.

Don’t create custom versions of the logo.

Please don’t rotate the logo.

Changing the typeface will make the cxpartners visual design department very sad.

You wouldn’t do this, would you?
